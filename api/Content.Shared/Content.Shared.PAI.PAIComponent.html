Class PAIComponent

pAIs, or Personal AIs, are essentially portable ghost role generators. In their current implementation in SS14, they create a ghost role anyone can access, and that a player can also "wipe" (reset/kick out player). Theoretically speaking pAIs are supposed to use a dedicated "offer and select" system, with the player holding the pAI being able to choose one of the ghosts in the round. This seems too complicated for an initial implementation, though, and there's not always enough players and ghost roles to justify it. All logic in PAISystem.
